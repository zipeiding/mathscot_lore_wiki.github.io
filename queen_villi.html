Queen Villi (kwi:n ˈvɪli), known as Lady Vilhelm (ˈleɪdi: ˈvɪlˌhɛlm) in physical form, is the Queen of the gods. She is the most powerful entity in the universe, with only the God of Evil rivaling her authority. She is the highest ranking member of the Council of Gods and is its leader.
