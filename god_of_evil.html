God of Evil

Monster of Evil redirects here.

The God of Evil is the cause of the forces of evil. It is the only entity powerful enough to challenge the authority of Queen Villi and the Council of Gods. In physical form, it is a colossal arachnoid figure that reaches nearly 15 meters, and it is known as the Monster of Evil. In spider form, he has been defeated two main times. Directly after the creation of the universe, he fought against the Spirits, and he was defeated after divine intervention by Lady Vilhelm. The place that the battle formed into The Pond, the most influential area of Earth. The second battle takes place within the cosmos, in the space in between the realms of the living and fallen. After this defeat, the Council of Gods try him, and they unanimously vote to convict him. He is sentenced to completely live out 1000 lifetimes as a mortal.
