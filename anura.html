Anura

Anura (ə.nʊr.ə) is a province in Western Ellipsia . It consists of multiple islands located in the Western portion of the Pond. It is bordered by Sobreka to the North, Fossilstan to the South, and Fishish Reservations to the East via maritime borders. It has over 10.7 million residents, making it the most populous province in Ellipsia.
