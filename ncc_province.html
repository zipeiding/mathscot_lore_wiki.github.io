New Capital City (Province)

Several terms redirect here. For the Capital City of the Ellipsia Republic, see New Capital City (Capital). For the historic city, see Midpoint Isle (Historic City).

New Capital City is a province in the Ellipsia Republic. It is the only province in Central Ellipsia and consists of one small island near the Center of the Pond. Within it lies New Capital City, the capital city of the Ellipsia Republic, as well as other municipalities. It is the only province to not have provincial borders, being only bordered by Fishish reservations to the North, West, South, and East. It has over 6 million residents, making it the eighth most populous province in Ellipsia.
