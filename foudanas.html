Foudanas

Foudanas (faʊ.dɑː.nəs) is a province in Western Ellipsia that lies on the Southern Coast. It is bordered by Fossilstan to the North through maritime borders, Scalebrook to the West, and Shellshore to the East. It has over 5 million residents, making it the eleventh most populous province in Ellipsia.
