Province

Provinces are regions of the Pond. They generally consist of areas with similar species living in it. Historically, they have been either part of a larger nation, especially the Amphibian Dominion, Holy State of Sancta Philitina, or Imperium, or had limited formal government. Trunkspire was the only province to have an independent governing system prior to the formation of the Ellipsia Republic. In the Ellipsia Republic, the provinces are the first level administrative divisions, and they each hold immense power due to the federal system of governance. There are a total of 18 provinces.
