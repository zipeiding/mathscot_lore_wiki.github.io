Arthronia

Arthronia (ɑːr.θroʊn.iə) or (ɑːr.θrɒn.iə) is a province in Southern Ellipsia that lies on the Southern Coast. It is bordered by Shellshore to the West, Trunkspire to the East, and Fishish Reservations to the North. It has over 660,000 residents, making it the least populous province in Ellipsia.
