Fossilstan

Fossilstan (fɑː.səl.stæn) or (fɒ.səl.stɑːn) is a province in Western Ellipsia . It consists of an island in the Western portion of the Pond. It is bordered by Anura to the North, Scalebrook and Foundanas to the South, and Fishish Reservations to the East. It has over 3.2 million residents, making it the 7th most populous province in Ellipsia.
