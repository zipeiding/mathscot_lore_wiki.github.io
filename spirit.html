Spirits

The Spirits are a group of divine, powerful entities that help shape and maintain the world. There are a total of thirteen spirits: The Spirit of Order and Balance, The Spirit of Peace, The Spirit of Chaos, The Spirit of the Sun, The Spirit of the Moon, The Spirit of the Stars, The Spirit of Land, The Spirit of Sea, The Spirit of Sky, the Spirit of Agriculture, The Spirit of Energy, The Spirit of Innovation, and The Spirit of Time.
