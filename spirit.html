Mathscots Lore Wiki

Spirits

The Spirits are a group of divine, powerful entities that help shape and maintain the world. There are a total of thirteen spirits: The Spirit of Order and Balance, The Spirit of Peace, The Spirit of Chaos, The Spirit of the Sun, The Spirit of the Moon, The Spirit of the Stars, The Spirit of Land, The Spirit of Sea, The Spirit of Sky, the Spirit of Agriculture, The Spirit of Energy, The Spirit of Innovation, and The Spirit of Time. They all have two main forms: A celestial, godly form, and a humanoid physical form. All of them are members of the Council of Gods, where they share equivalent power except for the higher ranking Spirit of Order and Balance.

Spirit of Order and Balance

The Spirit of Order and Balance is the most powerful spirit, responsible for maintaining stability and harmony in the Universe. The physical form of the spirit goes by the name ‘Lord Cayden.’

Spririt of Peace

The Spirit of Peace plays a crucial role in the Mathscot Universe, ensuring tranquility throughout the world. In humanoid form, he goes by the title ‘Lord Krish.’ The Spirit of Peace is known to clash with the Spirit of Chaos due to their differing desires on how to run the world.

Spirit of Chaos

The Spirit of Chaos imposes randomness and variance on the world. Its physical incarnation goes by the moniker ‘Lord Mayukh.’ While he often argues with the Spirit of Peace, they are both necessary in the running of the universe.

Spirit of the Sun

The Spirit of the Sun is the primary controller of the Sun, the star that the Earth orbits around. The Sun is the only star that isn’t mainly controlled by the Spirit of the Stars. In physical form, it goes by the name ‘Lord Wesley.’

Spirit of the Moon

The Spirit of the Moon is responsible for the Earth’s only naturally-forming satellite. In its physical incarnation, it goes by the alias ‘Lord Adhrith.’

Spirit of the Stars

The Spirit of the Stars is the head in regards to most stars. The only star not under its lead is the Sun, as it is under the Spirit of the Sun. ‘Lord Abhinav’ is his name when in humanoid form.

Spirit of Land

The Spirit of Land is responsible for the oversight of land on Earth. It leads a diverse area, filled with deserts, forests, grasslands, and more. In physical form, it takes on the title ‘Lord Sumedh.’

Spirit of the Sea

The Spirit of the Sea presides over bodies of water on Earth, from small pools in the mountains to the high seas separating the islands. ‘Lady Anagha’ is its name when it is in humanoid form.

Spirit of the Sky

The Spirit of the Sky has authority over the ether that covers Earth. The spirit controls the movements of the wind and clouds, bringing storms and thunder when angered. When elated and brimming with happiness, it tends to remove the clouds and fill the world with luminary rays of the sun. And during twilight, filling the sky with a kaleidoscope of coruscant stars. However, this rarely happens as the Spirit of the Sky is typically depressed. When incarnated into a humanoid figure, it goes by the name ‘Lord Thien-An.’

Sprit of Agriculture

The Spirit of Agriculture is primarily responsible for the growth and cultivation of crops. It, along with the Spirit of Energy, Spirit of Innovation, and Spirit of Time, were not part of the original nine spirits, and rather formed by Lady Vilhelm to flesh out the world. Its name in physical form is ‘Lord Zipei.’

Spirit of Energy

The Spirit of Energy is the spirit responsible for the forces acting upon the universe. It, along with the Spirits of Energy, Innovation, and Time, were not part of the original nine spirits, but added later by Lady Vilhelm. ‘Lord Ryan’ is its alias in humanoid form.

Spirit of Innovation

The Spirit of Innovation presides over technological advancement within the universe, especially Earth. As with the Spirits of Agriculture, Energy, and Time, it was added later by Lady Vilhelm. In its humanoid incarnation, it goes by the moniker ‘Lady Amelia.’

Spirit of Time

The Spirit of Time is responsible for the celestial clock that the universe abides by. It was formed by Lady Vilhelm to further expand the world, as was the Spirits of Agriculture, Energy, and Innovation. In physical form, it’s name is ‘Lord Benjamin.’
