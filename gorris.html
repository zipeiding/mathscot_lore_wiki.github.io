Gorris

Gorris (gɔːr.ɪs) is a province in Northern Ellipsia that lies on the Northern Coast. It is bordered by Kazarki to the West, Hoofhaven to the East, and Fishish Reservations to the South. It has over 2.3 million residents, making it the sixteenth most populous province in Ellipsia.
